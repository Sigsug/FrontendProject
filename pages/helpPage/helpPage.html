Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my flight?

You can change your flight through your booking management page. Additional fees may apply based on fare differences and change policies.

What is the baggage allowance?

Standard baggage allowance includes one carry-on bag (8kg) and one checked bag (23kg). Additional baggage can be purchased during booking or later.

When can I check in?

Online check-in opens 24 hours before departure and closes 3 hours before the flight. Airport check-in closes 1 hour before departure.
